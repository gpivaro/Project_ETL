How to use this application:



  • You can acess the interactive list of S&P500 by clicking here;


  • Or, access the closing prices for a given company by entering in the URL the company ticker, the start date and the end date /"company ticker"/"start date"/"end date" (e.g. /MMM/2011-05-03/2021-03-05);


  • Or, verify the first trade date as well as the sector name for a given company by entering the URL company ticker "company"/"company ticker" (e.g. /company/MMM).


    *For a json format of the data enter /json at the end of the URL.
